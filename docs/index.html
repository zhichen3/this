About Me

I'm currently a Master's student studying computational astrophysics in Stony Brook University. I'm currently working under Prof. Zingale on exploring how different reaction networks would impact the flame propagation speed on a neutron star during X-ray burst through an adaptive-mesh hydrodynamical code, called Castro . Before becoming a graduate student, I had two summer internships working in the astro-group at Brookhaven National Lab responsible for creating models for hydrogen intensity mapping and quantum astrometry.
